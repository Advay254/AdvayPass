TWA — assetlinks.json

After generating your APK on PWABuilder, it gives you a assetlinks.json file. Replace the placeholder at public/.well-known/assetlinks.json in your repo with that file and redeploy. That's it.

[{
  "relation": ["delegate_permission/common.handle_all_urls"],
  "target": {
    "namespace": "android_app",
    "package_name": "YOUR_PACKAGE_NAME_HERE",
    "sha256_cert_fingerprints": ["YOUR_SHA256_HERE"]
  }
}]

Served automatically at /.well-known/assetlinks.json — no env vars needed.
